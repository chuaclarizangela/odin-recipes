French Toast

Description

An extremely loved recipe that is made everywhere. A popular breakfast dish, this recipe can be whipped up in a few minutes.

Make this popular dessert today to find out why it is so beloved!

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Into a bowl, mix all the ingredients and set aside.
  2. Slice bread to 3/4 inch in width.
  3. Soak bread into the mixture.
  4. Fry bread until cooked.
  5. Top the cooked toast with fresh cream, fruits, and honey.
  6. Enjoy!
