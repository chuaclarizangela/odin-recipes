Fried Egg Rice

Description

A beloved Asian classic that is easy to set up. Prep and cook time under 10 minutes!

This simple breakfast idea is addicting and will make you cook this often.

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl of cooked rice
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Fry egg in oil and make sure the yolk will stay runny. Season with salt.
  2. Place egg on top of rice and add sesame oil and soy sauce.
  3. Mix and enjoy!
