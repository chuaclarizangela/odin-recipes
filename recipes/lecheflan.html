Leche Flan

Description

An extremely loved recipe that is traditionally made in the Philippines. Creamy and delectable, this Filipino treat has won the hearts of many people around the world.

Make this popular dessert today to find out why it is so beloved!

Ingredients:

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Prepare the egg yolks and mix until egg yolks are all cracked.
  2. Add condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract to the egg mixture and mix.
  3. Put the mixture through a sieve until smooth and set aside.
  4. Add sugar in the llanera and cook until sugar is light brown.
  5. Pour in egg and milk mixture into the llanera.
  6. Cover the top with aluminum and steam for 30 minutes.
  7. After steaming, let it cool and refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
  8. Enjoy!
